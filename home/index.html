ABOUT THE AI

A little bit of information about this wesbite.

Why

ShotAI originated as a grade 10 IB MYP personal project, blending a passion for basketball with the innovative potential of AI. Designed to enhance players' free-throw skills, our AI provides nuanced feedback that is both accessible to beginners and valuable to seasoned athletes. This project not only represents an academic challenge but also a practical solution to refine basketball techniques.

How It Works

Leveraging sophisticated AI algorithms, ShotAI meticulously analyzes uploaded video footage frame by frame. The system scrutinizes each aspect of a player’s free-throw motion, offering specific pointers for form adjustment and consistency.

Our Goal

Our mission is simple: to equip basketball players at every level with the tools to excel. ShotAI isn't just about correcting errors; it's about fostering an understanding of basketball mechanics, enabling players to improve their free-throw with each shot.

Innovation

Innovation drives ShotAI. We're committed to refining our AI through the latest in machine learning and real-world input, ensuring that the advice we give represents the forefront of sports training technology.

How to Use the AI

Get Ready to Record

Prepare to record your free-throw shot in a well-lit area. Ensure the camera is steady and captures your full profile from head to toe. This helps the AI in analyzing your posture, arm angle, and stance.

Upload Your Video

Navigate to the upload section on the ShotAI website. Click on the "Choose File" button and select the video you just recorded. Once selected, confirm the upload by clicking on the "Submit" button.

AI Analysis

Once your video is uploaded, ShotAI goes to work. The AI will break down your video frame by frame, analyzing your movements and comparing them with ideal free-throw mechanics.

Receive Personalized Feedback

After the analysis is complete, ShotAI will provide you with personalized feedback. This feedback will highlight areas of strength and aspects of your technique that could be improved.

Practice and Improve

Use the feedback to work on your free-throws. Practice the suggested improvements, and feel free to upload more videos to track your progress over time.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Some of the most asked questions.

How quickly will I receive feedback after uploading my video?

Your personalized feedback is typically generated within minutes after uploading your video, allowing for immediate practice and improvement.

Can I upload multiple videos at once for analysis?

While ShotAI is designed to analyze one video at a time for optimal feedback, you're welcome to upload videos back-to-back for a comprehensive training session.

How does ShotAI ensure the privacy of my videos?

Your privacy is our priority. Videos are processed securely and are not stored on our servers longer than necessary for analysis, ensuring your data is protected.

What makes ShotAI unique compared to other training apps?

ShotAI harnesses advanced AI specifically tuned for basketball free-throws, providing detailed, personalized feedback that's unparalleled in the sports training domain.
